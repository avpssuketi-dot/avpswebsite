About Angels Valley Public School

English Medium | Nursery to Class 8th

Angels Valley Public School, established under the visionary leadership of Founder Shri Raj Karan Singh and directed by Mr. Ashish Singh, is a progressive English Medium school located at SH 71, Indrani Nagar, Suketi, Fatehpur.

We are committed to providing quality education with a perfect balance of academic excellence, discipline, and moral values. Our aim is to nurture every child into a confident, responsible, and knowledgeable individual ready for the future.
